How the week works

One hour of input, one of practice, one of reflection — six times over.

Sixty participants, split into mentor teams of twenty and work groups of five. Each three-hour session follows the same rhythm, but the working method changes every time so the activity itself reinforces what the session teaches.

Runnable workshop tools

Sticky-note boards with dot-voting, column sorters, process maps with swimlanes, connection maps, the ICE simulation, and the dice & push/pull games — all saved per group.

Six sessions, six methods

Think-pair-share, gallery walk, role simulation, speed critique, game-based learning and World Café — chosen to reach different learning styles and keep the energy up.

Student & mentor views

Switch between the participant worksheet and the mentor view — sample answers, minute-by-minute run-of-show and the facilitator pack. Available in Norwegian and English.

Collect, share & print

Switch between groups, gather every group's work for the cross-team reflection, share between devices with a code, and print to PDF for plenary.
